## Methodology

This analysis uses **monthly-frequency cross-asset ratio analysis** to detect structural regime transitions over 1-2 year horizons.

**6 Components** (each scored 0-100 for transition signal strength):

1. **Market Concentration** (25%): RSP/SPY ratio - mega-cap concentration vs broadening
2. **Yield Curve** (20%): 10Y-2Y spread - interest rate cycle transitions
3. **Credit Conditions** (15%): HYG/LQD ratio - credit cycle risk appetite
4. **Size Factor** (15%): IWM/SPY ratio - small vs large cap rotation
5. **Equity-Bond** (15%): SPY/TLT ratio + correlation - stock-bond regime
6. **Sector Rotation** (10%): XLY/XLP ratio - cyclical vs defensive appetite

**Transition Detection** uses a 3-layer approach:

1. MA Crossover: 6-month vs 12-month SMA crossover on each ratio
2. Momentum Shift: 3-month ROC reversing against 12-month trend
3. Cross-Component: Multiple components signaling simultaneously

**5 Regime Classifications:** Concentration, Broadening, Contraction, Inflationary, Transitional
